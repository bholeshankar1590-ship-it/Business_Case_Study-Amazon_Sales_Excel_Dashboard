# Business_Case_Study-Amazon_Sales_Excel_Dashboard
1. North region dominates revenue, contributing the highest total sales (~3.65M), significantly outperforming all other regions.

2. West region is the second strongest market, indicating strong demand concentration in developed regions.

3. East and South regions are underperforming, highlighting clear opportunities for market expansion and targeted sales strategies.

4. Top 5 products contribute a large share of revenue, led by Nestle Smarties Pop-Up, showing strong product concentration and dependency.

5. Product portfolio is skewed, meaning a few products are driving most of the business — potential risk if demand shifts.

6. Top sales reps significantly outperform others, with Shari Silva leading by a notable margin (~408K), indicating performance gaps across the sales team.

7. Weekend sales (Saturday & Sunday) are highest, suggesting strong consumer buying behavior during non-working days.

8. Friday shows the lowest sales, indicating a mid-week dip before weekend spikes.

9. Sales are evenly distributed across weekdays, but no strong weekday peak — opportunity for targeted promotions.

10. Geographic + product alignment opportunity exists, meaning high-performing products can be pushed more in low-performing regions to boost revenue.
